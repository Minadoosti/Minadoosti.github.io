Starting my new position as a Chancellor's Fellow

I'm thrilled to share the wonderful news that I will start a new journey as a Chancellor's Fellow at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. This is a significant milestone in my academic career, as I will step into a more permanent role within this esteemed institution. I am extremely excited about contributing to the University's academic legacy, expanding my research horizons, and building my own research team in the company of my amazing friends and colleagues in my favourite city. Moreover, I am eagerly joining the recently established Quantum Software Lab in Edinburgh, and I am very happy for the opportunity to join my forces with the current efforts to elevate QSL towards becoming a globally renowned centre for quantum applications research.

As I embark on this exciting new chapter, I look forward to engaging in new exciting research projects and collaborations with both past and future colleagues. The prospect of working alongside exceptional students and interns within QSL, as well as those who will hopefully join us soon, ignites my enthusiasm and excitement.

I am immensely grateful to each and everyone who has supported me with kindness and patience along this path. Thank you for sharing in my excitement as I start this remarkable journey.
